### Requirements
- This is an android mobile app to help users to manage their expenses and finances
- The accounts part
    - The user will be able to add bank accounts, credit cards and cash in hand
    - This can be later used as a source for income, expenses, investments, paying off loans etc
- The expense tracking part
    - The user will be able to track expenses - date, source, amount, description etc
    - The source of the amount can be choosen from accounts
    - The expenses can be associated to some tags or categories to do some analysis later
- The income part
    - The user will get income from various sources and it can be credited any bank account
    - The sources can salary, investment returns, debt repayment etc
- Self bank account transfers. From one self account to another self account
- Also need to track investments, not the returns. It's like marking an expense but the amount will be invested to mutual funds, bonds, ETFs etc

### Next phase improvements
